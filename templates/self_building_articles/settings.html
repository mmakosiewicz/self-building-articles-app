GitHub mirror — an optional, continuous backup of your work-in-progress.

GitHub mirror

What this does: while enabled, the app continuously pushes your in-progress writing to a GitHub repo — on every scrap you add, every thesis edit, and every story regeneration. Per article it writes:

  • articles/<slug>/meta.json — title, thesis, status
  • articles/<slug>/story.md — the current "What's emerging" analysis
  • articles/<slug>/scraps.md — all scraps in one document
  • articles/<slug>/drafts.md — your drafted sections

Your raw notes and half-formed drafts end up in that repo, so point it at a private repo unless you want them public. Switch it off and nothing leaves this workspace — articles live in the local database either way.
